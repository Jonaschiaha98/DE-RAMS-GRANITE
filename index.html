ABOUT CAR LIFTS TECH

At car lifts technologies we have got outstanding electric and hydraulic car lifts. On our hydraulic car lifts, we have got both low rise, high rise and portable lifts. Our lifts consistently deliver on promise of breakthrough quality and unsurpassed engineering excellence. To deliver on our promise, we go through every effort to get our automotive lifts Sanas certified. Our mission is to supply the safest lifts. If you are looking for a car storage that doubles as a service lift, please contact us. We have High quality 4 post wheel alignment lift heavy duty capable of keeping up with the demands of a busy workshop. This have got a 4 ton lifting capacity.

More About Us

Installation service can be catered for which ever level you need of us. For multi lift installations, we can carry out full project management, handling hire of lifting equipment, waste disposal and multi drop deliveries. On the reasons customers chose us is that we can take responsibility for the complete project. At car lifts technology, we do not carry out on site electrical work, so it is the responsibility of the client to have their power supply ready for the installation.

At Cat lifts Technologies we offer a wide range of car Lifts from lifting capacity of 800kg up to 6500kg. You should equip your workshop with the appropriate lift. You should not lose business out of your malfunctioning or non-working equipment, call us now for a quick response.

Our lifts operate both on one or 3 phase power supply. Our equipment is from local (South Africa) Europe and China.
