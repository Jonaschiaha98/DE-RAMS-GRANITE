ABOUT DE RAMS GRANITE

De Rams Granite’s trust-based relationships with its customers have resulted in the company achieving the phenomenal feat of manufacturing more than 7 000 tombstones in a period of six years across southern Africa. De Rams Granite’s business philosophy prioritises the company’s commitment to assisting families in honouring their deceased in a dignified, culturally appropriate fashion.De Rams Granite enacts this philosophy by providing a wide variety of custom-designed tombstones and plaques at prices that accommodate virtually all income groups. In addition, the company offers customers all of the other products and services necessitated by a funeral ceremony, as well as funeral plans and ongoing maintenance of the gravesite.

Vision and mission

To be the tombstone manufacturer of choice throughout Africa.

To afford Africans opportunities to have their legacy carved in stone.

To be a world-class leader in the tombstone industry in terms of innovation and excellence.

To provide top quality products and services that accommodate all income groups.

To provide top quality products and services that accommodate all income groups. To offer first-rate customer service, whereby customers are treated with the utmost compassion and dignity.

To provide customers with a wide variety of options and that they would not be able to obtain elsewhere.

Services

We are looking forward giving you the best service. Make your choice of the Tomb services you want and let us do the work.

Grave locating and Tomb Building service

To ensure that our grace carers can correctly identify your loved one’s grave, you will need to provide the grave locations number. If you don’t have this, we can locate it for you for a once-off fee. Our dedicated team offers a compassionate and meticulous grave tending service designed specifically for these cherished moments.

Tombstone and plaque design

De Rams Granite’s skilled stone masons and other artisans are well-equipped to craft a tombstone exactly according to customers’ specifications. The company’s designers can intuitively provide with design ideas to create a unique tombstone or plaquard that honours the memory of your loved ones. Batuang Memorials’ tombstone offerings accommodate a wide range of budgets.

Memorial repairs

GraveCare will bring any damage to the gravesite to your attention, in the form of a detailed written and photographic report. Quotations can be provided for: Repairs to tombstones or plaque; Replacement of damaged or missing cases; Renewal of stone, pebble or slate chips; Regrassing the plot; Replenishing topsoil and other miscellaneous repairs.

Special dates

Customers can choose to have their loved one’s grave tended in time for significant dates such as birthdays or anniversaries. we understand that certain dates hold a profound significance in the lives of families and individuals.
